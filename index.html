❓ How to Use InvoiceForge

1Set Up Your Business (One Time Only)

Fill in your Business Name, Address, Email, Phone and GSTIN in the Business Details section. Upload your logo if you have one. Then click 💾 Save My Business — your details will auto-fill every time you open the file. You never have to enter them again.

Tip: Also fill in your Bank Details and UPI ID in the Notes & Payment section before saving. These will appear on every invoice automatically.
Bonus: Click 🪪 Business Card to generate a shareable digital visiting card from your business details — download as an image or share it directly via WhatsApp.

2Fill in Invoice Info

The Invoice Number is auto-generated in the format INV/2025-26/001 and increments automatically. The Invoice Date is set to today. Change either if needed.

3Add Client Details

Enter your client's Name, Email, Phone, Address and their GSTIN if they are a registered business. The PO/Reference Number is optional — use it if your client gave you a purchase order number.

4Add Line Items

Click + Add line item for each product or service you are billing. For each item fill in:

  • Description — what you provided
  • HSN/SAC code — optional, for GST compliance (e.g. 998314 for IT services)
  • Type — Service or Goods
  • Qty — quantity
  • Unit Price — price per unit

Amount is calculated automatically. Use ▲▼ arrows to reorder items.

5Set Tax & Discount

In the Tax & Settings section:

  • Choose Discount Type — Percentage or Flat Amount — and enter the value
  • Toggle GST Applicable on or off
  • Select IGST (inter-state) or CGST+SGST (intra-state supply)
  • Pick the GST slab — 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%
  • Select Place of Supply (the state where service is delivered)
IGST or CGST+SGST? If your client is in a different state — use IGST. If same state as you — use CGST+SGST.

6Download & Share

  • PDF — downloads a high-quality PDF ready to email or print
  • WhatsApp — downloads the PDF and opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Attach the PDF and send.
Every time you download or share, the invoice is automatically saved to your History.

7History & Daily Export

Click History in the top bar to see all invoices you have generated. At the end of each day, click Export to Excel inside History to download your full invoice register as a spreadsheet — date, client, amounts, GST, everything in one file.

⚠️ Important: History is stored in this browser only. If you clear browser data it will be lost. Export to Excel daily to keep a permanent record.

8Backup & Restore (Advanced)

Inside the History panel you will find two options — 💾 Backup Data and 📂 Restore Data. Use these only if you are switching to a new computer or a different browser.

  • Backup Data — downloads a small JSON file with your business profile, invoice counter, and settings
  • Restore Data — uploads that file back to recover everything on the new device
For daily use you do not need these. Just Export to Excel from History at the end of each day and your records are safe.
